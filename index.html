How We Work

1

We aim to make the customer experience as seamless and convenient as possible. To begin the process, simply fill out the booking form below. We understand that every project is unique, and we are more than happy to accommodate bespoke requests. Note that flying is weather dependent, so if possible, please provide a timeframe of when you need the job completed.

2

Once we receive your booking, our detailed preparation process begins. We conduct a thorough planning phase, including a "virtual survey" using satellite imaging and street view to assess the area and identify any potential obstacles or limitations. We also check the regulations in your area to obtain necessary approvals and get back to you with a quote.

3

With a flight plan created, we strategically choose the optimal day with favorable weather conditions and let you know when we'll be filming. Our skilled and experienced pilots take care of the filming according to your requirements, capturing the desired imagery with precision. The best part? You don't need to be on-site during the flight.

4

After the survey, our dedicated team securely handles the captured images and processes them according to your specific requirements. Whether it's editing, analysis, or any other extra service, we ensure that the final results meet your expectations. We transfer the processed files via secure online file transfer methods, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of your data.
